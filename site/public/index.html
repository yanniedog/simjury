The daily ritual

Listen closely. Choose honestly. Say why.

  1. 01Hear the case

    Read or listen to a compact 2026 dispute and track the moments that move you.

  2. 02Lock your verdict

    Commit before anyone else speaks. No hints, rewinds, or answer-shopping.

  3. 03Face the room

    Choose the evidence you would argue. The seeded jury may agree, split, or hang.

The human question

Facts are rarely the only thing on trial.

Memory. Motive. Fairness. Doubt. Each case is invented, but the judgment it asks of you is real: what did you notice, what did you assume, and could you explain your choice to a room of strangers?

One case is enough. There is no feed to clear. When today's room closes, come back tomorrow if you want another seat.
